Project Description

SaveUp is an Android Java application designed to empower users to manage their finances efficiently. It offers income and expense tracking, budgeting tools, goal setting, and robust security features, all while providing a user-friendly experience to help users achieve their financial objectives.

For income management, users can effortlessly record various sources of revenue, ranging from regular salary payments to occasional bonuses and other income streams. Each entry allows users to specify the amount, date, and even add supplementary notes, ensuring a thorough record of their financial inflow. Expense tracking within SaveUp is equally comprehensive and user-centric. Users can categorize their expenses into predefined categories.
