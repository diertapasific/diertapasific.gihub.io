Project Description

The Push News Hub app is a dynamic Android Java application that revolutionizes the way users receive news updates. Powered by Firebase's push notification capabilities, this app ensures that you never miss an important headline or breaking story. Whenever significant news breaks, you'll receive instant push notifications directly on your device, keeping you in the loop, no matter where you are. But that's just the beginning. The app also provides access to a vast repository of news articles, offering an extensive library of 100 news stories sourced from APIs. With a user-friendly interface, you can easily browse and explore these articles, staying informed about the latest developments in a variety of areas.
