Project Description

One of the standout features of the app is the season/year schedule, which offers users a detailed overview of the F1 racing calendar. Fans can easily access race dates, locations, and timings, ensuring they are well-informed about when and where their beloved Ferrari team will be competing.

What sets this app apart is the seamless integration of ticket purchasing directly from the schedule page. Users can secure their tickets to watch Ferrari and other F1 teams in action without the hassle of navigating multiple platforms. Furthermore, the app also offers a dedicated transaction history page, providing users with a transparent view of their ticket purchases and financial transactions made through the app.
